Chicken Caesar Salad

⏳ Preparation Time: 15 mins 📊 Difficulty: Easy 🔴 Category: Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 2 grilled chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar salad dressing

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly, then chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place the chopped lettuce into a large serving bowl.
  3. Add the sliced grilled chicken, croutons, and Parmesan cheese on top of the lettuce.
  4. Drizzle the Caesar dressing evenly over the salad ingredients.
  5. Toss the salad gently until everything is evenly coated with the dressing.
  6. Serve immediately as a light and refreshing meal.
