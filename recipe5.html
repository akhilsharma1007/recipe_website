Classic Cheeseburger

⏳ Preparation Time: 20 mins 📊 Difficulty: Medium 🟢 Category: Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 mix)
  • 4 hamburger buns, toasted
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties slightly larger than the buns.
  2. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a grill or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the lettuce, tomato, and cheesy patties on the toasted buns.
